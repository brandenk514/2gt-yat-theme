THE YOUTUBE CHANNEL

We take very seriously the trust our viewers put in that our content is honest and truthful. Our commitment is to our viewers and community first; we will always attempt to have a non-biased viewpoint on all videos we produce. Every video is completely written by 2GuysTek, and we maintain full editorial control. No company can pay for a preferential review. We choose to produce videos based on newsworthiness, personal interest, or based on our use of those products. As part of the video creation process, we may communicate with manufacturers of the products & software covered on our channel and ask them to explain behaviors or comment on product deficiencies so they can share their side of the story. Our policy is that no vendor, sponsor, or individual is given a video preview before publication. Much of the hardware we review on our YouTube channel is purchased by 2GuysTek either for review or as part of a project we are working on. Any hardware or software provided by the manufacturer for us to review is disclosed as such in the video. Videos containing sponsored content are explicitly labeled in the video’s description and verbally within the video itself. It will always be clear what is being sponsored and the business relationship between the sponsor and 2GuysTek. We are a US-based company and comply with the laws set forth by the FTC: FTC Disclosures 101 for Social Media Influencers

Google AdSense Advertisements

Google’s AdSense platform selects the advertisements placed on our YouTube videos, and we have no input or affiliation with the ads or the companies that produce them.

Amazon Affiliate Links

We participate in the Amazon Associates program, which allows us to make a commission on product links we add to the description and comments of our videos. We only provide affiliate links to Amazon for products we’ve purchased or used within the video the links are included with.

eBay Affiliate Links

We participate in the eBay affiliates program, which allows us to make a commission on product links we add to the description and comments of our videos. We only provide affiliate links to eBay for products we’ve purchased or used within the video the links are included with.
